Texas Sheet Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 squares Butter
  • 8 Tablespoon of Cocoa
  • 2 Cups of flour
  • 2 Cups of Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of bakingsoda
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1/2 cup of Buttermilk
  • 5 tablespoon of Milk
  • 1 tablespoon of Karo
  • 6 cups of powdered sugar

In a heavy sauce pan - melt together:

  • 1 square Butter
  • 4 T Cocoa
  • 1 Cup Water

In a large mixing bowl sift together:

  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 tsp. Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp. Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp. Salt

Then add chocolate mixture to dry ingredients. Adding:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp. Vanilla
  • 1/2 Cup Buttermilk

Pour into a greased and floured jelly roll pan. Bake at 350* for 18 minutes.

Chocolate Frosting

Using the same heavy sauce pan - melt together:
  • 1 square Butter
  • 4 T Cocoa
  • 5 T Milk
  • 1 T Karo syrup (not necessary but makes it a little creamy and DD like)
  • 6 Cups (or 2 lbs) Powdered Sugar (add more until creamy)
Beat until smooth, spread evenly on cooled cake
